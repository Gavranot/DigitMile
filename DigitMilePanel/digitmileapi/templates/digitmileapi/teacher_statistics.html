Understanding the Metrics

  • Accuracy: Average percentage of correct moves across all games
  • Average Score: Mean score achieved per game
  • Win Rate: Percentage of games where students achieved first place
  • Decision Speed: Average time per move (inverted: faster = higher on chart)
  • Engagement: Number of games played per student (higher = more engaged)

Interpretation: Larger radar areas indicate better overall performance. Look for classrooms that excel in specific metrics versus those with well-rounded performance across all areas.
