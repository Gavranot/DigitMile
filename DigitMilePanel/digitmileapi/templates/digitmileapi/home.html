Welcome to DigitMile

DigitMile is an educational platform that helps teachers create classrooms, track student progress, and manage learning through an interactive game-based experience.

For Teachers

If you're a teacher and want to use DigitMile, you need to register first. Make sure your school is already registered in our system. If not, contact your school administration to register the school before you sign up.

For School Administrators

School administrators can register their institution to enable teachers to join the platform. Once approved, your teachers will be able to create classrooms and add students.

Create Classrooms & Add Students

Once you're logged in as a teacher, you can create classrooms and add students. Each student will be automatically registered in the system and can start playing the game to learn and improve their skills. You'll be able to track their progress through detailed statistics.
