DigitMile Game

An Interactive Educational Experience

How to Play

DigitMile is an educational game designed to help students improve their mathematical skills through interactive gameplay.

Getting Started

  • Ask your teacher for your classroom key
  • Enter the classroom key when prompted
  • Select your name from the student list
  • Start playing and learning!

Game Controls

  • Use your mouse to interact with game elements
  • Follow on-screen instructions
  • Complete challenges to earn points
Need Help? Contact your teacher if you're having trouble accessing the game or if you forgot your classroom key.

Access Information

This game is part of the DigitMile educational platform managed by your teacher.

For Students

  • You must be registered by your teacher
  • Use the classroom key provided by your teacher
  • Your progress is automatically tracked
  • Teachers can view your statistics and performance

For Teachers

  • Log in to the teacher panel to manage classrooms
  • Create classrooms and add students
  • Generate classroom keys for student access
  • View detailed statistics and progress reports
Teacher Portal Teachers can access the management panel at /panel/ to create classrooms and track student progress.

About DigitMile

DigitMile combines engaging gameplay with educational content to create a fun learning environment. Students progress through various levels, solving mathematical challenges while competing in an interactive game format.

Teachers can monitor student progress in real-time, viewing detailed statistics including accuracy rates, completion times, and learning curves. This data-driven approach helps identify students who may need additional support or are ready for more advanced challenges.

The game is designed to be accessible and enjoyable for students of all skill levels, adapting to individual learning paces while maintaining engagement through competitive elements and achievement tracking.
